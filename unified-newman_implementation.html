The Current Friction

  • ▪️Lost Links & Logins: Users constantly forget URLs for support, email, and Canvas, leading to Helpdesk calls.
  • ▪️Manual Resets: Helpdesk overwhelmed by simple password resets, delaying actual IT support.
  • ▪️Basic Ticketing: Freshdesk lacks Entra ID sync, forcing manual HR/IT data entry and blind asset management.
✨

The Unified Solution

  • ▪️Frictionless Workspaces: Centrally managed browsers pre-load all essential apps automatically on login.
  • ▪️Automated SSPR: Users reset their own 15-char passwords via our new interactive training app.
  • ▪️SolarWinds ITSM: The central hub that tracks assets, automates onboarding, and secures the network.

The 30-Day Transformation Track

We do not need months to see ROI. By unifying these initiatives, we deliver immediate, campus-wide results. Click any phase to expand.

Phase 1: The Quick Wins & Foundation

Days 1 - 7

Canvas SSO, Edge Policies Push, and SolarWinds Procurement.

  • ✅ Identity: Configure and launch Canvas SSO integration with Entra ID.
  • ✅ Workspace: Push Microsoft Edge Enterprise Policies via Intune (Startup pages, Auto-Login, Managed Bookmarks, Training Hub).
  • ✅ Procurement: Finalize SolarWinds Service Desk & Dameware licensing.

Phase 2: The Security Shift

Days 8 - 14

Launch Interactive Training & Enforce 15-Char Passwords.

  • ✅ Rollout: Deploy the interactive "IT Survival Guide" app to all staff and students.
  • ✅ Enforcement: Activate the 15-character password mandate in Entra ID.
  • ✅ Self-Service: Users enroll in SSPR, deflecting hundreds of initial password reset calls from the Helpdesk.

Phase 3: ITSM & GenAI Activation

Days 15 - 21

SolarWinds Core goes live; AI Features Enabled.

  • ✅ Cutover: Migrate active tickets from Freshdesk to SolarWinds.
  • ✅ User Portal: Launch the new Student/Faculty Self-Service portal with AI Quick Filters.
  • ✅ AI Assist: Activate GenAI Agent Communication, Data Masking on attachments, and Problem Suggestion.

Phase 4: The Automated Campus

Days 22 - 30

Advanced Workflows, Zero-Day Onboarding, Asset Integration.

  • ✅ HR Integration: Build Service Catalog workflows for Zero-Day Onboarding and Offboarding (Entra ID Sync).
  • ✅ ITAM Dashboard: CFO/IT review the populated Hardware Asset dashboard for budget planning.
  • ✅ Dameware: Standardize remote support across the campus, retiring unsecure third-party tools.
